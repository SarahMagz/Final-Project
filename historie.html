Location

Located on the Amazonis quadrangle, close to the the Martian equator, lies Zentopia. Zentopia is a highly modern and advanced city, functioning as a safe-space for our refugees from Earth. The city itself is divided into multiple quarters, each matching the different continents and cultures from Earth. The city center functions as a big melting pot, binding all of these cultures together and exposing them to one another.

Zentopia is a place where you are able to enjoy a tropical environment the rest of your life. The climate is made by pure advanced NASA technology. It is therefore possible to grow all kinds of fruit and vegetables all year round. The green scenery makes the air feel fresh and clean. The landscape varies from where you are located at Zentopia. Some locations can remind you of the beautiful beaches on the Maldives, green scenarios of the forest in Hawaii or the modern city of Singapore.

Landing

There are seven landing platforms located outside the city, one for each continent. Within a short time after arrival, you will be transported to the city thanks to our highly intelligent, self-driven buggy-cars.

When you first set your feet on Mars, you will soon come to realize that it’s not that different from your old home after all. Thanks to our breakthrough technologies, we’ve managed to terraform the Red Planet into a place of wonders.

Things to do

The city of Zentopia welcomes you with a place to live, and a job specifically tailored to your best abilities. In your spare time, you could visit one of our multiple stores, parks or museums. You could share a Martian Stirring Pot with your friends at Marn in Downtown Zentopia, take a stroll in the exotic Zingen-park, visit the Planet Tower or enjoy a take-away coffee from one of the multiple Coffa-shops around town.

We have brought the best cuisine from the earth to Zentopia. You are therefore able to enjoy, French, Spanish, Chinese or Italian food. All food made in Zentpoia are organic and cruelty free. With help from 3D technology we are able to produce enough food for all citizens without harming the environment.

Education is highly valued at Zentopia and it is therefore important to find your abilities early. The education system is made to find what your qualities are and push you towards the right profession. The core job opportunities at Zentopia will be within technology. It is important to obtain the knowledge within this field to create and maintain Zentopia.

Accommodation

Before you arrive to Zentopia, you will be able to book a beautiful accommodation tailored for your needs. There are small, but modern flats located in the heart of Zentopia specially made for young people and students. These new design flats are nicely furnished with a vegetable garden located on the rooftop of the building. This makes it possible to grow your own organic vegetables. You can also book a house by the coastline created for people with family. These great houses are surrounded by a flowery garden where your children can play and enjoy the peaceful life of Zentopia.

We have brought the best and most advanced new technology to Zentopia. All accommodations come with a tablet where you can control all technological equipment in the house/flat. You can control lights, alarm system and electricity from the tablet.

To make your life a bit easier we have made it possible for you to order robots to help you with every day tasks. If you have some spare money left there are options to order your own robot chef, cleaning robot and robotsitter.
