Location

Located on the Amazonis quadrangle, close to the the Martian equator, lies Zentopia. Zentopia is a highly modern and advanced city, functioning as a safe-space for our refugees from Earth. The city itself is divided into multiple quarters, each matching the different continents and cultures from Earth. The city center functions as a big melting pot, binding all of these cultures together and exposing them to one another. Before you arrive to Zentopia, you will be able to book a beautiful accommodation tailored for your needs. There are small, but modern flats located in the heart of Zentopia specially made for young people and students. These new design flats are nicely furnished with a vegetable garden located on the rooftop of the building. This makes it possible to grow your own organic vegetables. You can also book a house by the coastline created for people with family. These great houses are surrounded by a flowery garden where your children can play and enjoy the peaceful life of Zentopia. We have brought the best and most advanced new technology to Zentopia. All accommodations come with a tablet where you can control all technological equipment in the house/flat. You can control lights, alarm system and electricity from the tablet. All houses and flats are made with a sophisticated interior design feature. All accommodations are originally built with white and clean walls with big windows, but can be altered to each person’s style. You can therefore choose pictures and colours on the walls after your own desires with your housetablet. The colours will change from white to blue directly after you have chosen the colour you want on the tablet. To make your life a bit easier we have made it possible for you to order robots to help you with every day tasks. If you have some spare money left there are options to order your own robot chef, cleaning robot and robotsitter.

Landing

There are seven landing platforms located outside the city, one for each continent. Within a short time after arrival, you will be transported to the city thanks to our highly intelligent, self-driven buggy-cars.

When you first set your feet on Mars, you will soon come to realize that it’s not that different from your old home after all. Thanks to our breakthrough technologies, we’ve managed to terraform the Red Planet into a place of wonders.

Things to do

The city of Zentopia welcomes you with a place to live, and a job specifically tailored to your best abilities. In your spare time, you could visit one of our multiple stores, parks or museums. You could share a Martian Stirring Pot with your friends at Marn in Downtown Zentopia, take a stroll in the exotic Zingen-park, visit the Planet Tower or enjoy a take-away coffee from one of the multiple Coffa-shops around town.
