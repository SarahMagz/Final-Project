FAQ


How long does it take from Earth to Mars?

Answer: The journey takes between 150-300 days depending on the alignment of Earth and Mars, the speed of the launch and the amount of fuel used for the trip.


How much luggage am I allowed to bring?

Answer: The maximum weight for luggage is 30kg (66 lb). If you need to exceed this limit, please contact us via e-mail.


Am I allowed to bring my pet?

Answer: Unfortunately we have to prioritize humans. Pets are currently not allowed on flights because of the risk of spreading deseases. Pets can however be adopted at shelters in Zentopia.


Can I return to Earth if i go to Mars?

Answer: No, the journey to Zentopia is a one way trip.


Can I go to Mars if I'm pregnant?

Answer: Yes. Zentopia is still in need of health personnel, so there are always doctors and nurses available on our flights in case something happens.
